Mutually speak.
Mutually listen.
Mutually learn.

We believe the voices of local residents most adversely affected by issues in their communities should be central to global discourse.

The GALV site will connect people around the world with similar societal interests and enable them to have face to face real time dialogue about the well being of their communities. This will be a free open source tool to teach about your life in your community and learn from others about their communities, through open dialogue or in-depth interviews.

Our goal is to make this online communication as private and secure as technology and our available resources allow.

Direct and personal interaction has the power to inspire greater empathy, transformative relationships, and collaborative organizing for positive sustainable change.

Please refer to our mission statement for more information.
