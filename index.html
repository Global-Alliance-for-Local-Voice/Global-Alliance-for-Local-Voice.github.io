Global Alliance for Local Voice (GALV) is a nonprofit launching a website devoted to enabling people around the world with similar sociopolitical concerns to have face to face, private, online dialogue about the well being of their communities.

GALV's site is a free tool to teach people about your life in your community and learn from others about their communities.

We believe informative dialogue will inspire greater empathy, transformative friendships, and collaborative organizing for positive sustainable change.
