GALV (Global Alliance for Local Voice) is a nonprofit based in Seattle, Washington devoted to sustaining an open source platform that will encourage people to learn about societal issues and places around the world by hearing from local residents living with those issues daily. We strive to reduce the human disconnect between our world's population and its globalized relationships by cultivating personal interaction online and transformative global relationships.
